# Account & Sign-in

You can use the entire terminal **signed out** — everything is stored locally in your browser. Signing
in adds **cloud sync** and **Rewards**.

## Ways to sign in

Click **Sign in** (top-right) and choose:

| Method | What happens |
| --- | --- |
| **Google** | Standard Google OAuth. Your identity is your Gmail. |
| **Wallet** (Web3) | Connect MetaMask / Phantom etc. Your identity is your wallet address. |

{% hint style="info" %}
**Wallet sign-in** works even when a full cloud session can't be established — it still registers you for
**Rewards** (points, referral link, leaderboard) using your wallet address as the account.
{% endhint %}

## What signing in unlocks

* **Cloud sync** — your **search history** and **Private Bookmarks** follow you across devices.
* **Rewards** — you start earning **points** for using features, get a **referral link**, and appear on
  the **leaderboard**. See [Rewards](track/rewards.md).

## Privacy & anti-abuse

* The terminal uses a **device fingerprint** (a hash of browser/hardware characteristics) to keep the
  rewards system fair — **one device = one account**. It is **not** your MAC address (browsers can't read
  that) and isn't used to track you across the web.
* If a device is already linked to another account, a second sign-in on it is declined for rewards
  (you'll see a notice) — this prevents point farming.

## Signing out

Use **Sign out** in the account menu. Locally-stored data (bookmarks made while signed out, the early
paper book) remains in your browser unless cleared.
