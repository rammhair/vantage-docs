# Private Bookmarks

**Track → Private Bookmarks** is your personal saved list of tokens.

## How to use it

* **★ (star)** any signal card or holder row — or a token in the workspace — to add it here.
* Open **Track → Private Bookmarks** to see your saved tokens with their composite read at a glance.
* Click any to jump back into its [Token Workspace](../research/token-workspace.md).

## Sync

* **Signed out:** bookmarks are stored **locally** in your browser.
* **Signed in:** bookmarks **sync to the cloud** and follow you across devices. See
  [Account & Sign-in](../account.md).

{% hint style="info" %}
Bookmarks are private to your account. Use them as a focused watchlist of tokens you're actively tracking.
{% endhint %}
